Academic Magnet High School (AMHS) is a Magnet School for academically gifted individuals.

AMHS was rewarded the title for #1 school in the nation in 2019, and #2 in 2020.






It is interesting to compare Academic Magnet High School SAT scores to those displayed in this interactive visual. The SAT scoring in 2013 was out of 2400 points, whereas for the most recent years it has changed to 1600 points. The average SAT score for the chart is about 800 points less than perfect, whereas, at Academic Magnet, the average score from last year is less than 300 points under the perfect score.
